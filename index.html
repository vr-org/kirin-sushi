Our Story

Where Japanese
tradition meets
signature craft

At Kirin Sushi, we believe exceptional Japanese cuisine should be experienced without limits. Every plate arrives as a small work of art — precise, generous, alive with flavour.

Born from a deep respect for Japanese culinary tradition and a bold creative spirit, Kirin is more than a restaurant. It is an experience, repeated and rediscovered with every visit.

The Space

Designed for
atmosphere

Warm lanterns, dark timber screens, and intimate lighting create a space that feels both theatrical and inviting. A setting built to make the meal unforgettable.

Located in the heart of Vila Nova de Gaia, Kirin Sushi welcomes you every day for lunch and dinner — reservations recommended.

The Experience

What makes
Kirin different

01 Unlimited Rodizio

Order freely from the full menu, as many times as you like. No limits, no rush — just pure enjoyment of Japanese cuisine at its finest.

02 Signature Craft

Each dish is prepared with care and precision. Premium ingredients, artistic plating, and bold flavour combinations define every plate.
